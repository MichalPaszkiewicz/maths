Algebra and fractions

1. Multiply out and simplify the following: \( (a + b)(a - b) \)

Worth: half a chocolate.

2. Simplify the following: \( \frac{1}{(x + 7)} + \frac{1}{(x - 7)} \)

Worth: half a chocolate

3. Simplify the following: \( \frac{5}{x} + \frac{3}{(x + \frac{49}{x})} \)

Worth: one chocolate
